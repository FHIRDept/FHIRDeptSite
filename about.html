About Us

Our story, our mission, and our people.

Our Mission

To build a resilient community where every individual has the opportunity to thrive.

Founded in 2010, Community Hope began as a small group of neighbors concerned about the lack of after-school programs in our area. What started in a living room has grown into a multifaceted organization serving hundreds of families each year.

We believe that change happens when people come together. By pooling our resources, time, and talents, we can address the unique challenges our neighborhood faces, from food insecurity to educational gaps.
