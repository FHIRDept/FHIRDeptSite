Our Mission

The FHIR Department is a community dedicated to fostering collaboration, knowledge sharing, and innovation around the Fast Healthcare Interoperability Resources (FHIR) standard. Our mission is to reduce barriers and foster innovation to harness the transformative potential of FHIR in healthcare.

Community Building

Activities to create and mature the FHIR Community by providing a space for intelligent conversations.

Learning

Platform for healthcare professionals, developers, and other stakeholders to learn about FHIR best practices, explore its capabilities, and share their expertise.

Implementing

Place for developers and implementers to share experiences, troubleshoot issues, and develop solutions for common FHIR implementation challenges
